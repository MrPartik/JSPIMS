  Opening Rights of 2018 Founding and Charter Anniversary Celebration   Love conquers every tempest in a teacup  
         
  The Catanduanes State University (CSU) led by Dr. Minerva I. Morales, SUC President III, opens its month-long celebration, October 15, 2018, of this year’s Founding and Charter Anniversary Celebration. The following were the activities in the opening rites: Doxology (HablonDawani), Flag Raising (National and CSU flags), PanunumpasaWatawat, Community Singing (Catanduanes Beloved, CSU Hymn, SerbisyoSibil), Panunumpa ng Kawani ng Gobyerno, Opening Salvo (HablonDawani), Field Demonstration (Laboratory Schools), Picture Taking, Unveiling of ILOVECSU Marker and New Building Markers, Blessing of New Buildings, Ground Breaking of CAS Building –B, and Opening Program in the afternoon at the auditorium of the 5th Philippine International Biodiversity Meeting and 3rd International Conference on Science Education and Technology (BIOME5 & SEDTEC3). The event was likewise graced by the presence of Board of Regents, Hon. Jerry T. Ang, and Hon. Carmen Francisco, Usec. Gloria Jumamil –Mercado, Hon. Mayor Sammy V. Laynes of Virac, Hon. Vice-Governor, Shirley A. Abundo, VP-AA, Dr. Maria Tugano, VP-REPA, Dr. Lily P. Custodio, and former CSU-VP, Dr. Aida Dianela.

This year’s commemorative event has its theme, “ Bridging Times, Sustaining Excellence, Transforming Lives.”

Upcoming events include: Family Day of all CSU officials, faculty, and non-teaching personnel at Mamangal Beach resort, Virac on October 18, 2018; Diana of the CSU Marching Band around the campus on October 19, 2018 (morning), and GawadParangal for Outstanding CSU Alumni starting 4:00 o’clock in the afternoon at the auditorium; Jobs Fair on October 23, 2018 at the auditorium; Open House and Green Pa More in the last week of October 2018.		   ILOVECSU” concrete marker was unveiled, October 15, 2018, epitomizing gratefulness of the heart that speaks only such gratitude and reverence to the nurturing embrace of one’s alma mater.

The activity preceded the blessing and unveiling of building markers led by CSU president, Dr. Minerva I. Morales, in the newly completed learning infrastructure facilities– unprecedented in recent memories, and dedicated to the pursuit of quality education in the province of Catanduanes, namely:

  • College of Business and Accountancy (CBA) Building
  • College of Information and Communications Technology (CICT) Building
  • Engineering Laboratory Building
  • Entrepreneurial Development Center
  • Gymnasium
  • Auditorium
  • College Of Arts And Sciences (CAS) Academic Building- B (Ground Breaking)

“ILOVECSU” Let it be forever in a grateful heart because the language of love always endures and lies supreme
