SERVICES

 

 
 
Processing of Application for College Entrance Examination
 
REQUIREMENTS:
 
Senior High Graduates
1. Duly accomplished College Entrance Examination Form (CSU-F-GCTO-02)
2. Official Receipt of payment for College Entrance Examination Fee
3. One (1) photocopy of Student Report Card (original copy to be shown)
4. One (1) photocopy of Certificate of Good Moral Character
5. One (1)brown envelope, long
6. One (1)folder, long
7. Two (2)2 x 2 ID pictures
 
Graduating Senior High Students
1. Duly accomplished College Entrance Examination Form (CSU-F-GCTO-02)
2. One (1) brown envelope, long
3. One (1) folder, long
4. Two (2)2 x 2 ID pictures
 
Transferees
1. Duly accomplished College Entrance Examination Form (CSU-F-GCTO-02)
2. One (1) photocopy of Transcript of Records or Certification of General Weighted
Average in previous course taken (original copy to be shown)
3. One (1) photocopy of Certificate of Good Moral
4. One (1) photocopy of Honorable Dismissal
5. One (1) brown envelope, long
6. One (1) folder, long
7. Two (2) 2 x 2 ID pictures
 
Students Shifting Courses
1. Grades/Evaluation of subjects with General Weighted Average in previous course
taken, signed by College Dean or Program Adviser (original copy to be shown)
2. Endorsement from the Dean of College where student was previously enrolled.
3. One (1) Photocopy of Certificate of Good Moral Character
4. Two (2) 2 x 2 ID pictures with white background
5. One (1) folder, long
 
 
Administration of College Entrance Examination
 
REQUIREMENTS:
College Entrance Examination Permit/Admission Slip (CSU-F-GCT-01)
 
 
 
Counseling Services
 
REQUIREMENTS:
Referral Slip from Faculty Member/Staff (CSU-F-GCTO-05B for referred confidential cases; CSU-F-GCTO-05A for student cases)
 
 
Issuance of Certificate of Good Moral Character
 
Requirement:
Student ID
